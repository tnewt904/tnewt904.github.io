Our Story

My name CLiff Benson and I started Benson Surfboards in my garage when I was 22. I always loved to surf but was frequently dissapointed with the quality or design of the boards I was riding. I decided to start making my own surfboards and never looked back. As a creative individual I attempt to present my surfboards in an artistic way that emulates the beauty in surfing. My original designs first caught the attention of friends and family, and eventually spread to local surfers from the North Carolina coast. My increasing popularity fueled me to start a business based out of Raleigh, North Carolina with another shop in Wilminton, North Carolina. Ultimately, Benson Surfboards are completely customizable, down to the colors, lengths, and fins. Surf with style, outlaws.
