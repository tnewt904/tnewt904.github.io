Frequently Asked Questions

Question 1: How long will a board take to make?

It depends on the lenth and style of the board. Most boards take me between 20-30 hours of work.

The board will be wrapped in bubblewrap and foam to protect against damage. The board will arrive at your doorstep within 5-8 business days after completion. Shipping is free!

The boards are almost completely customizable. I can make them any color, a variety of lenghts, and can change fin confiurations to anything you desire.
